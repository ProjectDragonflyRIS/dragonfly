FAQ

1) How is this particularly different from other existing products like this?
As previously mentioned, this is a product that not only will factually change how we use vehicles and how they operate, but is more adaptive both towards the environment and society's economy.
2) How will you receive funding for this project?
Well, there are several ways with which we will be able to get this project funded, the simplest of which being: sell it to investors and let them take it from there. Another option would be to partner with existing companies that may benefit from this, and use their funding in order to create the first functional dragonfly machine.
3) What are the long term results to switching to this type of vehicle for major shipping businesses?
We bring together modern technology into every day lives via environmentally healthy resources and the ability to save as much materials as possible, since you will no longer need seperate vehicles for different forms of travel, exploration, and shipping.

It may be hard to believe, but if we all collectively began using this vehicle in place of planes, helicopters, trucks, and ships, which mind you all are conventionally powered by oils, we would have a plethora of freed metals and resource. These may be recycled and reused for other purposes, which will allow economies and industries grow, as critical demands for certain things will drop.
4) What is the Dragonfly Modular Customization System©?
Our drone can be customized to the clients will, they may choose the size and which uses the drone/vehicle may have. For example, some drones may be used for rescues or others may be used only for cargo.

The ability to change the functionality and size of the vehicle moves us a crucial step into transportation and delivery capabilities.
5) What caused the spark to this project?
This is the result of our participation in the FIRST® LEGO® League, we used critical thinking to our advantage to reach where we are now.
6) What is the First® Lego® League?
First® LEGO® League introduces science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) to children ages 4-16* through fun, exciting hands-on learning. Participants gain real-world problem solving experience through a guided, global robotics program, helping today's students and teachers build a better future together.

Our three divisions inspire youth to experiment and grow their critical thinking, coding, and design skills through hands-on STEM learning and robotics.
